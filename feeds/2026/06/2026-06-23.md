  - Source: Invincible Awesome Loop Engineering / invincible04/awesome-loop-engineering
  - Category: Loop prompt libraries
  - Status: external
  - Why it matters: A **loop contract** is the whole loop written down as data: goal, trigger, intake, workspace (and what's *off-limits*), verification gates, memory, budget, escalation, and exit.
